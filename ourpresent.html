Our Present

 Ang ating bansang Pilipinas ay maraming napagdaanan. Iba't-ibang banyagang kultura ang nakapag-impluwensya sa ating bansa. Na kahit na tayo ay isang malayang bayan na, marami parin ang naiwan nila sa atin. Gawi, ugali, relihiyon, at lengwahe at marami pang iba. 

 Sabayang Pagbigkas 

 "Wikang Pangkasalukuyan"

  

  

 Our world, the Earth has also experienced a lot of changes during its 4.5 billion years of existence. It once has dinosaurs, mammoths, rare plants, and other amazing creatures that has gone extinct now. It's because of natural causes, and also the emergence of us humans. Since the early days of our ancestors up until now, we have been reliant to nature. To the resources that this Earth has been providing, and we have been abusing it. Because of these activities, natural disasters have been happening too frequently as the health of our environment decline.
